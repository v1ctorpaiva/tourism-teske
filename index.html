Lake Louise, Alberta
Lake Louise is a hamlet in Banff National Park in the Canadian Rockies, known for its turquoise, glacier-fed lake ringed by high peaks and overlooked by a stately chateau.

Toronto, Ontario
Toronto is the capital city of the Canadian province of Ontario. With a recorded population of 2,731,571 in 2016, it is the most populous city in Canada and the fourth most populous city in North America.

Vancouver, British Columbia
Vancouver is a major city in western Canada, located in the Lower Mainland region of British Columbia.
